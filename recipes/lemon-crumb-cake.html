Lemon Crumb Cake

lemon cake on bottom lemon curd in center mmmm

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • lemon zest
  • salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan. Set aside.
  2. Make crumb topping by combining 1 1/2 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, 2 tablespoons lemon zest, 1 teaspoon lemon juice
